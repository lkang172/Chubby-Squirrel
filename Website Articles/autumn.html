Autumn

Pictures and Article By Chubby Squirrel

Last Wednesday, the 22nd of September, marked the first day of Autumn (at least for me)! Although I am very indecisive and can't choose any one of the seasons as my favorite, I definitely enjoy the fall a lot. Here on the United States east coast, the leaves on trees turn develop vibrant colors, and mountains become more picturesque than ever. The weather gets a bit cooler, so that if you get up early in the morning, you may have to don a sweater, but not so much so that you need to turn on the heat. In any case, fall is a wonderful season, and in this article, you will learn new things about it!
